About Infinity Travel

Infinity Travel is a travel agency headquartered in Abu Dhabi, UAE, serving individual travelers, groups, and corporate clients. The company positions itself around personalised, high-touch service rather than one-size-fits-all booking, pairing modern travel technology with a focus on client satisfaction. It has been recognised with industry awards as a leading travel agent in Abu Dhabi.

In a message from the Managing Director, Ubairis, the company describes its outlook on travel as a way of connecting distant places and different people — treating tourism less as a transaction and more as a craft, and aiming to be a long-term travel partner for its clients rather than a one-time booking service.
